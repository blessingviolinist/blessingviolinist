About Blessing

Trained. Passionate.
Unforgettable.

Blessing Martins — also known as BlessingViolinist — is a multi-genre professional violinist, musician, music educator, advocate, and paraprofessional. Born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria, he moved to Boston, Massachusetts to pursue a Master's degree in Music Education at Boston University.

Blessing earned a Bachelor of Arts in Music from the University of Lagos, a Diploma in Violin Performance from the Musical Society of Nigeria (MUSON), ABRSM Grade 8 in violin performance, Grade 7 in theory, and holds a Level 1 Suzuki violin teacher certification.

More than just a performer, Blessing is a dedicated music advocate with a deep commitment to inclusive education. His work with students with disabilities — including co-teaching at the MPHS special education program — reflects his belief that music has the power to unlock hidden talent and serve as a meaningful form of therapy.

  • M.M. Music Education — Boston University
  • B.A. Music — University of Lagos, Nigeria
  • Diploma in Violin Performance — MUSON
  • ABRSM Grade 8 Violin · Grade 7 Theory
  • Level 1 Suzuki Violin Teacher
  • MUSON School of Music Quest Youth Competition Winner
  • Special Educator & Paraprofessional
